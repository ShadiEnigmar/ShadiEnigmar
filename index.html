About Me

Jared Mason

Jared Mason is a student at the Pennsylvania State University studying Digital Art and Media Design. As a student, he produces digital work that deals primarily with 3D modeling, animation, and game design using software such as Blender, Unity, and the Adobe Creative Cloud. After college Jared intends to follow a career as a digital artist for the video game industry.

These experiences showed him that everyone has their own value when it comes to making a group task successful.

In his spare time Jared plays a wide variety of video games. At least he did. Then he started playing League of Legends and it became the game he chooses to play ninety percent of the time. He also enjoys watching old cartoons and listening to new music.
