About

Jared Mason is a Digital Art and Media Design student at the Pennsylvania State University. He produces work in 3D modeling, animation, and game design. After college Jared intends to follow a career as a digital artist for the video game industry.

Jared’s time as a Boy Scout (now Eagle Scout), Club Secretary, and SIGGRAPH student volunteer allowed him to work closely with a wide array of different people. SIGGRAPH, being a computer graphics conference with over 18,000 attendees, allowed him to assist and interact with professionals in the digital media industry. These experiences showed him that everyone has their own value when it comes to making a group task successful.

In his spare time Jared plays a wide variety of video games. He also enjoys watching old cartoons and listening to new music.
