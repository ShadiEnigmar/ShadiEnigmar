About

I am an undergraduate at The Pennsylvania State University who is pursuing a degree in Digital Arts and Media Design. With my work, I am interested in creating interactive experiences that others can relate to. I believe that interactive media is the most effective way to convey ideas or emotions to an audience.
My current work focusses on game design, vector artwork, or 3D modeling. I often incorporate multiple of these aspects into one project in order to create unique and interesting experiences. My artwork often focuses on ideals I believe to be important. While creating my artwork, I try to include ideals that are important to me. Common themes in my work include environmentalism and the unrewarding nature of the standard work life.
