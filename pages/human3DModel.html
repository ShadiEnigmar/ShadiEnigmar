Human Model



Character designed, modeled, textured, rigged, and animated over the course of a semester.
She is supposed to represent a city girl who has grown tired of all the pollution that modern life brings. In response to this, she has decided to leave on a journey into the forest. However, she has never been before and is incredibly underprepared.

2020, Blender and Surface Painter
