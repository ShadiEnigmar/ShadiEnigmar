PoliCity

Created by Priya Jurasinski and Jared Mason

Policity, a mobile phone game, demonstrates the complexity of decision-making pertaining to our world. In this app, players must swipe across the screen to view different areas of a city while looking for glowing objects. The glowing objects can be clicked on, which leads to a question regarding the city. Players answer the question, which impacts the world slightly. Overtime, these slight changes add up to produce an entirely different looking world by the end of the game.

Made with OpenFrameworks using Android Studios. Digital Art and Media Design team at Penn State's SoVA.
